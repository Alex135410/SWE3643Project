PSO is an intuitive app connecting college students with mentors, including peers, professionals, and college organizations, facilitating personal and professional growth. With easy filtering options and a user-friendly interface, PSO empowers students to forge meaningful connections and access valuable guidance for academic and career success.

About

PSO is a dynamic application designed to seamlessly connect students within college communities for mentorship opportunities. Whether seeking guidance from peers, seasoned professionals in various industries, or college organizations, PSO facilitates meaningful connections to foster personal and professional growth. Through its user-friendly interface, students can easily browse and filter mentors based on their areas of expertise, interests, and availability. PSO's robust platform encourages collaborative learning, networking, and skill development, empowering students to navigate their academic journey with confidence. Whether it's seeking career advice, academic support, or extracurricular involvement, PSO serves as the go-to hub for students to forge valuable connections and unlock their full potential.

Features

Customizable Profiles

Users can create detailed profiles highlighting their expertise, interests, availability, and preferred communication methods to ensure effective mentor matches

Matching Algorithm

The app employs a sophisticated matching algorithm that suggests compatible mentor pairs based on shared interests, goals, and backgrounds

Messaging Platform

Integrated messaging allows seamless communication between mentors and students, allowing them to schedule meetings, ask questions, and exchange advice.

Event Calendar

A calendar feature where users can discover and RSVP to mentorship events, workshops, and networking sessions hosted by the organizations and professionals.
